With Business Carbon Calculator, small businesses can measure their carbon footprint and report reduction progress to value chain stakeholders, such as customers or investors.

Pillars of our calculator

  • Ease of use

    No previous experience required, we guide you through finding and adding all the information you need to make an estimate of your business’s carbon footprint.

  • Coverage

    We calculate your business’s footprint from direct emissions (scope 1), indirect (scope 2) as well as some key expense areas (scope 3).

  • Actionability

    Identify emission hotspots and pursue your carbon reduction journey with the SME Climate Hub. Find the information you need to update your value chain stakeholders (like investors or customers) on your progress.

  • Trust

    Normative is an official partner of the UN-backed SME Climate Hub. Our calculator is built with support from Google.org in accordance with the Greenhouse Gas Protocol standard. Read more about methodology.
